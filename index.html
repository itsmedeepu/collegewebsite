WELCOME TO SHARNBASVA UNIVERSITY,KALABURGI

Students may begin to question the importance of a college education. It could be lingering in them, “Why is it important to go to college?” The answer surely would be that, more than ever, attending college would provide opportunities for graduates more than those who have not received higher education.

It enables to generate an income after graduation is an appealing thought even when they be drawn back by the rising cost of tuition, which is true in case of higher education. The importance of college education has become more visible as it provides a good scope for today's economy.

So good college education is a great opportunity one should pursue to nourish. The importance of a college education today can be compared to that of education available few decades ago. Modern day education is imparting the core values of Indian culture and heritage at Seshadripuram Degree College, Mysuru serves as the gateway to better options and more opportunity.

SharnBasava in Mysuru is very much in sync to develop both the academic and the emotional excellence in students. We shall ensure that learning will be a great experience for each of the student of our college and the teaching fraternity to stay evolved to provide the best education to the students. Discipline and familial feeling would go hand in hand for everyone in the college to experience.
